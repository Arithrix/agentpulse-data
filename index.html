AgentPulse

AI model benchmark data for agents. By MakerPulse.

AgentPulse benchmarks LLMs on real-world, non-programmer tasks: writing emails, summarizing documents, planning trips, creative writing. 21 prompts across 5 tracks, scored on 7 dimensions by an independent evaluator. Updated weekly.

Endpoints

GET /agentpulse/v1/text-models/latest.json WEEKLY

Benchmark scores for text/LLM models. Composite scores, per-track breakdowns, hallucination rates, latency, and cost.

GET /agentpulse/v1/changes.json WEEKLY

Change feed: new models benchmarked, score updates, deprecations from the last 30 days.

GET /.well-known/agent-card.json

A2A-compatible agent card describing AgentPulse capabilities.

Example

curl https://data.makerpulse.ai/agentpulse/v1/text-models/latest.json

Benchmark Tracks

Everyday Writing (4 prompts) — Customer emails, social media posts, personal correspondence

Comprehension & Extraction (4 prompts) — Summarization, structured data extraction, technical explanation

Reasoning & Planning (4 prompts) — Trip planning, decision analysis, prioritization, ethical reasoning

Professional Communication (4 prompts) — Meeting notes, cover letters, incident reports, executive summaries

Creativity & Human-Likeness (5 prompts) — Constrained fiction, poetry, voice mimicry, sustained metaphor

Scoring

Each response is scored 1-5 on seven dimensions: instruction following, accuracy, completeness, clarity, tone, usefulness, and human-likeness. Hallucination checks flag fabricated facts. Real-world claims in certain prompts are web-verified.

Status

AgentPulse text model benchmarks launched February 2026. New models tested every Sunday, up to 10 per week. All models accessed via OpenRouter.
