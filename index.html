AgentPulse

Structured AI service data for agents. By MakerPulse.

AgentPulse provides machine-readable pricing, uptime, and quality benchmark data for AI services. Designed for AI agents that need to make informed decisions about which services to use.

Endpoints

GET /agentpulse/v1/image-generation/latest.json DAILY

Unified pricing, uptime, and benchmark data for AI image generation services.

GET /agentpulse/v1/changes.json DAILY

Change feed: price changes, uptime alerts, new benchmarks from the last 30 days.

GET /.well-known/agent-card.json

A2A-compatible agent card describing AgentPulse capabilities.

Example

curl https://data.makerpulse.ai/agentpulse/v1/image-generation/latest.json

Currently Tracking

Image generation services: Replicate (Flux Schnell, Flux Pro), OpenAI (DALL-E 3), Stability AI (SD 3.5), Google (Imagen 3), Black Forest Labs (Flux Pro Direct), Together AI (Flux Schnell).
